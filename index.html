WELCOME!

On this Monstercat Album Browser, you can browse tracklists from Monstercat's compilation albums and navigate through them. Click on each track to play them right on this page, or click the genre circles on the top right corner to filter the tracklist by track genres. Right now you can browse through all compilation albums except 019 and 020. Expect LPs and probably EPs soon!

This album browser is inspired by /u/Bloq's Visual Tracklist from Reddit, go check it out! If you have some feedback about this project feel free to tell me at /u/gamehelp16 or comment on this project's blog post!
